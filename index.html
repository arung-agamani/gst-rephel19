playing games, theatre perform, murals and canvas painting, and some activities i have never seen before

it was nice. this town looks very lively

that very night, i went to the east side of the town, looking for an inn

22 september 1719



today i went to the west side of the town in search for this bar

the guy who plays accordion was there, giving performance on the side of the town hall

food stalls were increasing, as i could smell the rich but mixed flavor of every single food on the town hall

probably because it was sunday, more people were gathering on the town hall.


"krrrrrrrr"


yeah it was my stomach growling

it could not be helped. i have not eaten anything since i woke up

last night incident made me awake longer than usual. i mean it was not everyday that someone infiltrates your room on random inn

i wonder if i was stalked ever since i entered this town. well could be it, could be not.



i approached the closest food stall there, trying to fill my belly with something


"One beef rolls please!"


"Here you go. You must be new here!"


"Ahh yes. i arrived yesterday."

the beef rolls were pretty tasty, and worth my 4 small copper coins


"By the way, have you gone to the west side of the town? It is a good place for tourists like you"


"Well, actually i am looking for someone there. I was told to go to Black Rose Bar, to search the 'Old loyal customer' there."



soon after i said that, the cook stopped for a moment. i could also feel that my heart stopped for a moment in anticipation


"you reeaalllly need some patience if you are looking for him", he replied with low tone


what is this? is he some kind of important person or what?

"i could assure you that he is not going to be there this day. if you are really need to meet him, find someone who his left arm is always open."


"As another tips, he once made a game to educate kids about math. People love his game, despite the fact that the game was only the product of his spare time for assignment on his first semester on university."


"it is going to be a challenge. i suggest to make a search party.", he closed as another customer came to buy

i was sure that this was not going to be easy, right on that moment

Earlier this morning, I woke up with some sore in my back.

"Eh, what is this?

At this point I don't have any choices rather than to follow this piece of information



East hall, 5th main building

He will only be there from 10am to 11am. Be quick


...

Soon after that, I ran towards the east hall.

The fifth building, huh?

Well, there were 4 main buildings on the sides of the town hall

The fifth and the sixth on the west side, the seventh and the eight on the east side

24 september 1719



I started this morning with even more questions left unanswered in my head.

Yesterday was hectic. We managed to find the memorial statue with the unsolved mystery attached to it. It turned out to be some morse codes which leads us to another riddle. It seems that the riddlemaster didn't want the riddle to just be solved by some random passerby.

We were requested to gather on the "field full of love" by 9am. He said he was going to give us something in return of a beer I bought him yesterday. Old people really like to return a favor, eh?

...

We went to the southern side of the town to restock our supplies. I told my crews that we're probably not going to leave this town soon. There is something interesting going on here.

Upon hearing that, several cheered, several groaned, most of them being silent as usual. My assistant seemed okay with my decision, so I won't comment much. Maybe they would discover something interesting for them here, sooner or later. I recalled that on the first few days I arrived here, things almost became messy. Well, probably because I am the captain. Important people is important, heh.

...

We met the old loyal customer on the said "field full of love". Thank god, he didn't forget about this. We were dead waiting for him to come. I didn't really feel something lovely from that field, or park. It was nice, though. The scenery was nice, it was clean, and there wasn't much people there. A pretty peaceful place to meet someone. Probably that's why it's called "field full of love".


Turns out, he always goes to this field every 9am, in remnants of his wife. The old loyal customer gave us a map of Genshiken town. It's different from the map we got the first time. We kept the map in the stash to keep it safe.


He told as about the stories of Genshiken, about how he was a war veteran. We were interested in one particular story. The story about the statue of a woman in the memorial site. The story about a statue that changes it's position every several hours. The story about a statue that also faces different direction with each pose. The story about a statue which itself is telling a story.


We were so curious that we decided to observe the statue. It's changing over time, so I told several of my crews to periodically observe the statue.





The story seemed trying to tell a message, but we had no idea where to start. The story tells different story depending on where we start. A tricky one.


We were told to be cautious on choosing our way. It wasn't without reason, because every choice can only be attempted once per day. We definitely don't want to waste our time here.



A moment to think...
